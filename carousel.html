THIS PROJECT

Call to all buzzlievers! Across the United States, over 44 percent of honey bee colonies have been lost from Spring of 2015 to 2016. Bees are reponsible for 30% of the world's food crops-food we eat as well as foraging crops. Cross-pollination accounts for the growth of 90% of wild plants. It is caused by the Colony Collapse Disorder, a phenomenon caused by global warming, the loss of habitat, invasive parasites, and pesticide exposure. It is crucial that we wake up as a nation, and take on the reposnibilty of our role of nature's keeper for our sake and of all living things. We not only present a platform to advocate for the bees , but virtual reality gaming! Experience the life of a Bee!!
